Kyoto in a minute

Kyoto is the ancient capital of Japan and a city of a thousand temples. Here, tradition meets nature: shrines, tea houses, bamboo groves, and picturesque streets preserve the spirit of old Japan.

  • 1200+ years of history
  • 17 UNESCO sites
  • 200 festivals a year
  • 2000+ temples and shrines
  • 1000+ places serving authentic cuisine
